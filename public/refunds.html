Refund Policy

Last Updated: June 3, 2026

Thank you for subscribing to CEFR Stories. We want to ensure you are fully satisfied with our CEFR reading platform. Since AI models incur real-time operational costs, we have established the following refund conditions:

1. Eligibility Window

You can request a full refund within 14 days of your initial purchase or billing date, provided your usage does not exceed the limit specified in Section 2.

2. AI Usage Limit

To prevent platform abuse and cover API provider execution costs, refunds are only eligible if you have generated fewer than 10 story chapters in total since subscribing.

3. Refund Ineligibility

Refunds will not be issued if:

  • You request a refund more than 14 days after the billing transaction date.
  • You have generated 10 or more story chapters during the subscription term.
  • Your account is suspended or terminated due to a violation of our Terms of Service.

4. Processing via Paddle

All subscription payments, invoicing, and refunds are processed securely by Paddle, our payment Reseller. You can trigger a refund request or view billing help by clicking on the links provided in your Paddle receipt email, or contacting us with your Paddle Order ID.

5. Contact Us

If you meet the eligibility criteria and wish to request a refund, please send us your account details and Paddle invoice transaction reference to admin@teacherjake.com. We process eligible requests within 3 to 5 business days.
